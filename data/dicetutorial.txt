
 Welcome to the dice tutorial


 The dice game have 3 parts:

 1: Random number

 2: Counting the number of times pressed

 3: Secret (be carefull)


 Random number

 The random number is usefull, The number can be until 30 for default(can be changed)

 That number appear in the upper part on the black part of the screen


 Counting the number of times pressed 

 This can be found on the title or in the bottom part of the black screen


 Be carefull this is usefull for the secret part of the dice game
